Data Analysis for a Smartwatch Company
(Business Expansion)

We are a company launching a health tracker smartwatch into the market. We already have one office in Los Angeles, California, and things are going well. We have found investors to help us grow. Our young but promising startup has set its sights on expanding its operations to a new state other than California. This is an exciting opportunity for us to broaden our reach and tap into new markets. However, this expansion requires careful consideration of various factors, such as the economic, political, and social conditions of the target state. To ensure a successful expansion, we have decided to conduct thorough research and data analysis to identify the most suitable location. Our team will collect and analyze data on key metrics such as population size, property prices, and corruption index.

Our goal is to identify the state with the best potential for business expansion.
